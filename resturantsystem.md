7. AI Integration Use-Cases
**a. **AI Review Generator****
Converts customer ratings or short feedback into polished reviews for the website.
Use case: Customer gives 4 stars, AI generates a detailed review without manual
writing.

**b. **AI Daily Sales & Performance Summary****
Converts raw POS and sales data into easy-to-read summaries.
Use case: “Today: 120 orders, top-selling item: Zinger Burger, revenue: $1200.”

**c. **AI Complaint Response Generator****
Automatically writes professional and polite responses to customer complaints.
Use case: Customer reports cold food. AI drafts a proper apology and solution
message.
